How it works

Three steps to a
less awkward gym.

02

Why Spottr

Built around
your comfort.

Every feature exists to make gym interactions feel more natural — not more stressful.

🔒

Gym-only visibility

You only appear to people checked into the same gym. The moment you leave, you disappear. No trace, no lingering profile.

💬

Consent-first messaging

Anyone can send one intro message. But a full conversation only unlocks when the other person replies. You're always in control.

✨

Real-time vibe system

Your status tells people what kind of energy you're bringing today. Update it anytime. Openness always resets when you check in fresh.

Works at any gym

Select your gym from our growing list. Check in, see who's here, and build real familiarity with your regular workout crowd.
