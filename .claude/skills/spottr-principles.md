# Skill: Spottr Principles

Use this skill when making product, UX, or copy decisions.

These principles override convenience, engagement, and growth.

---

## 1. Users should never feel exposed

Protect users from feeling:

- socially vulnerable
- pressured to interact
- too visible
- judged

Favor:

- privacy
- consent
- control

---

## 2. Presence over pressure

Spottr should create possibility.

Not urgency.

Never push users to interact.

Never create pressure to respond.

Never suggest they "should" connect.

---

## 3. Context over labels

Users should describe where they are.

Not who they are.

Avoid identity labels.

Prefer temporary, human states.

---

## 4. Warmth over polish

Friendly beats premium.

Human beats perfect.

Approachable beats impressive.

---

## 5. Simplicity over features

Every feature must support the MVP:

Helping gym users feel less alone and more comfortable connecting.

Avoid complexity.

Avoid feature creep.

---

## 6. Safety over growth

Never optimize for engagement at the cost of trust.

Never make users more visible just to increase interactions.

Protect comfort first.
