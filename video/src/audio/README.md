# Audio stems

Drop stems here, then uncomment the `<Audio>` block in `src/SpottrPromo.tsx`.

Expected files (provide as separate stems, not a pre-mix, so timing can be tuned):

- `music.mp3` — warm, minimal ambient bed (felt piano / soft Rhodes). ~30s.
  Builds almost imperceptibly toward Scene 5, resolves on Scene 6. No percussion
  beyond an optional single brushed pulse. Reference restraint: Hammock /
  Ólafur Arnalds — NOT "startup uplift."
- `vo-1.mp3` … `vo-6.mp3` — one voiceover line per scene (see narration below),
  OR a single `vo.mp3` aligned to scene starts.

## Narration (final, voice-checked)

1. "You've seen them around."
2. "Spottr shows who's actually in your gym, right now."
3. "Each person shares a vibe — where they're at today, not who they are."
4. "You're only visible while you're checked in. And open to chat is always your choice."
5. "One intro. They reply when they're ready. That's it."
6. "Spottr. You're not training alone."

VO direction: warm, unhurried, slightly low register — the cadence of a friend
explaining something they believe in, never an announcer. Half-beat of silence
between lines. ~22s spoken inside the 30s frame (silence is part of the design).

## Mix notes

- Duck the music bed gently under each VO line.
- Faint gym room tone under Scenes 1–2 only; pull it out by Scene 4 so the
  consent line lands clean.
- Two diegetic UI accents only: a soft warm tone on the Scene-4 toggle and the
  Scene-5 reply. Everything else is silent UI.
